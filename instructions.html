INSTRUCTIONS: In this study, you are being asked to choose between two words that will appear at the top right and top left of the screen. Choose the word that best describes you using the computer mouse.

INSTRUCTIONS: At the beginning of each trial, a box reading "START" will appear at the bottom center of the screen. To begin the trial, you will click the box. After a brief moment, two words will appear at the top of the screen. You should move the computer mouse to the word that you prefer. Once you begin moving the computer mouse, please keep the mouse moving smoothly and continuously until you've made your choice. You can change the direction and course of your mouse, but it is important that you keep the mouse moving.

INSTRUCTIONS: To give you practice, you will first complete 10 practice trials. For these trials, you will be presented with two single-digit numbers. Click the START button and then once the numbers appear, use the mouse to select the larger of the two numbers.
